I am a postdoc at Research Institute for Mathematical Sciences, Kyoto University in Japan.
My previous postdoc was at Institute of Mathematics for Industry, Kyushu University.
Before that, I was a PhD student of Dominic Verity and Ross Street at Macquarie University in Sydney, Australia.

I am interested in (higher) category theory.

Here (no. 8) is my 5 minute self-introduction from the Higher Categories and Categorificaction program at the MSRI in spring 2020.

I organised Kyoto Category Theory Meeting in February 2025.
